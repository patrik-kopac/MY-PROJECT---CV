Why IT?

At first, I’m an introvert guy with logical thinking, which kind of predestines me to be a programmer a bit. Secondly, for whole my life I’m circling around IT. My brother is an IT project manager for a long time, also my sister-in-law. I worked in IT firms in marketing department, so I know the enviroment. Also in marketing itself I found a few connections with IT (web analytics, making scripts etc.). I also have a few experiences with editing HTML code at Shoptet platform for two eshops I created (not working anymore). And at last, I just love to create websites and play with the code, can’t help myself. That’s why I’m doing ENGETO front-end developer academy right now (ending 20th of December) so I can improve in FE area.

Why Czech Republic?

I always liked and admired Czech Republic, even don’t know why. In 2011 I moved to CZE (Znojmo, Zlín, Chomutov) to play ice hockey professionally, because here were better conditions and better league for playing. After three years of playing and after my back injury I stayed in CZE for most of the time since then. I studied in Brno for a while and have worked and lived in Brno, Prague, Vsetín and now in Uherské Hradiště. Meanwhile I’ve found my wife here and I like Czech’s economic and politic situation more then in Slovakia for now, so it’s possible, that I’m gonna stay in Czech Republic for the rest of my life. We’ll see...
